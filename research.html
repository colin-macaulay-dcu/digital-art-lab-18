It is both a research intervention and a practical tool for teachers. The research sits within the context of the new Irish Primary Curriculum — particularly its emphasis on creativity, agency, inclusion, and the development of contemporary competencies in Visual Arts education. Unlike traditional research that tests a finished product, DBR develops the intervention and the knowledge simultaneously — meaning the DAL platform is shaped by what teachers say, do, and discover in real workshops throughout the research process.
